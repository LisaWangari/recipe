What is Ginger & white chocolate cake

Ingredients

3 cups flour.

250 g butter.

1/2 cup sugar.

1 tsp vanilla flavor.

Pinch salt.

1 egg.

1 tsp baking powder.

Cooking Method.

Cream butter and sugar together and remain a tbsp of the sugar for sprinkling later.

Add your vanilla flavor and egg into the cream and continue mixing.

Sieve the flour and baking powder.

Into the cream, add the flour, baking powder and salt and continue mixing.

Mould to form a dough and transfer to your baking board to form a rectangle.

Lay a nylon, sprinkle the remaining sugar on it and gently roll the cookies dough on it after which you wrap with the nylon and refrigerate for about 10mins to harden a little so it can be easy to cut into shape.

Slice the dough.

In a preheated oven, bake for about 10 minutes.

Serve with any drink of your choice. Enjoy and thank me later.

Goes well with...

  • Tea
  • Any choice of your own.
